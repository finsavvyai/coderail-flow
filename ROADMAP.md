## 🎯 Competitive Positioning

### vs. Selenium/Playwright
**Their weakness**: Requires coding, manual script maintenance
**Our advantage**: Visual mapper, no code needed

### vs. TestRigor/Mabl
**Their weakness**: Expensive, complex, testing-focused
**Our advantage**: Simple, documentation-focused, affordable

### vs. Loom/Screen Recording
**Their weakness**: Manual recording, no automation, no subtitles
**Our advantage**: Automated, programmable, always up-to-date

### vs. Scribe/Iorad
**Their weakness**: Screenshot-only, no video, limited customization
**Our advantage**: Video + screenshots, highly customizable, Cloudflare-powered

## 🚧 Known Risks & Mitigations

### Risk 1: Cloudflare Browser Rendering Limits
**Impact**: High
**Mitigation**: Monitor usage, contact Cloudflare early for limit increase

### Risk 2: Element Locator Reliability
**Impact**: Medium
**Mitigation**: Multiple fallback strategies, user feedback loop

### Risk 3: User Adoption
**Impact**: High
**Mitigation**: Visual mapper (killer feature), great onboarding, free tier

### Risk 4: Competition
**Impact**: Medium
**Mitigation**: Move fast, build community, unique positioning
